CHARACTERIZATION OF ADVANCED COMPOSITES


Abstract: As part of this project-based course taught by Dr. R. Byron Pipes, I manufactured 350 test specimens with individual specifications using HexPly 8852 CF material. Tested each specimen according to diverse testing methods, with compliance to ASTM standards. Post-processed (DIC and MATLAB) and performed FEA simulation (ABAQUS) to characterize each specimen for failure mode and convergence analysis.


Brief Summary

  • Autoclave curing of 40 HexPly Laminates according to ASTM specifications
  • Extract 350 specimens using tabbing, waterjet and surface grinder
  • Engrave notches and holes in 90 specimens for open-hole testing using CNC
  • Perform diverse tests to characterize different loading effects in composites
  • Extract data using DIC and perform FEA simulation (ABAQUS) for comparison
